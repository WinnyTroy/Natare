About Us

PHYSICAL LOCATION

Catholic Diocese of Ngong lies within the archdiocese of Nairobi in Southern Kenya. It operates in the southern part of Rift-Valley counties of Kajiado and Narok with an approximate area of 39,619kmÂ² and a population of 1,538,232people (Census, 2009). The Diocese has 33 parishes divided into 4 deaneries (Kilgoris, Narok, Ngong and Loitokitok).

VISION STATEMENT

A loving community of God living fulfilled lives.

MISSION STATEMENT

To sustainably improve on the quality of life of people living within the Diocese by spreading the Good News of Christ and facilitating social-economic empowerment in justice and peace.

CORE VALUES/PRINCIPLES

  • Championing Togetherness in the Family of God
  • Facilitating Justice for all
  • Empowerment for communities
  • Enabling participation by/for all
  • Encouraging dialogue, peace and social communication
  • Living life's of witness
  • Ministering to the total person
