LIVELIHOODS

The Catholic Diocese of Ngong Livelihoods office has embarked on a project addressing community managed water projects in Kajiado County, where two water management communities will benefit. The project that is financed by Caritas Switzerland and the Water Integrity Network and supported by the Ministry of Water Kajiado County, is also carried out in Wajir and Marsabit Counties with an aim of training community groups managing small scale water supply systems through an ‘Integrity & Management Toolbox’ over a period of 6-9 months.
